About Us

Information Technology Specializing in Cyber Security · SLIIT · 2025–2026

We are a team of four final-year undergraduate students from the Department of Computer Systems Engineering, Faculty of Computing, Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT). Our research focuses on building a Resilient and Trust-Based OTA Firmware Update Framework for IoT Devices, bringing a unified, multi-layered approach that integrates Zero Trust authorization, intelligent firmware delivery, real-time device health validation, and fail-safe post-update resilience. The framework ensures that only trusted, healthy IoT devices receive secure and efficient updates while preventing compromised devices from accessing the OTA pipeline.
